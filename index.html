About Us

We are a relief foundation for homeless animals.

BabyLaikaInu is one of the first charity tokens on the BSC. Every single transaction has a 5% automatic liquidity protocol and a 5% redistribution protocol to protect holders. This protocol pairing increases the value of each holder’s investment over time. All dogs deserve a home. Unfortunately, not all dogs have a home. Our token was created to help homeless dogs.

We all remember LAIKA, the first dog to go into space on a mission.She was a dog from the street. One more of the many that every day they run into the path of people, looking for something to eat or simply waiting for a caress. But who found Laika wandering around Moscow was not a pedestrian who was moved to see her, but a member of the Soviet armed forces who captured her to turn her, without her knowing, into a "Space Dog".

This four-legged pioneer did not return. He became the first "victim" of space adventures, topping a list that would grow over the years with other animals.

An app to help animals.

We are developing a mobile application where people can adopt dogs rescued from the street or that have suffered abuse by their former owners.Today there are many abandoned animals that lead a life of hardships and difficulties. They are not to blame, the fault is the lack of compassion of the human being.

We as a foundation want to help these defenseless animals. We are organizing ourselves to cover as many countries as possible and have a headquarters in each of them, so that everyone can access the adoption of animals that have suffered a lot and only want a family that loves them.

We invite you to be part of this project if you love animals and like us you feel sad when an animal is suffering.
